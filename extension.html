Extension - Web App (2017)

With this project we focused on creating a solid pipeline before the requirement planning stages in order to ensure a solid foundation. In our project we aimed to create a virtual receptionist website, where buisnesses would have the ability for their customers to make appointments through various platforms they already use. Our web application has the ability for customers to make appointments through facebook messenger as well as SMS, and will recieve notifications previous to their appointments. Buisnesses can also create their own forms with required information to be gathered from their customers, checkin their customers, and see statistics of relating to appointments.
GitHub Repo Live Website

Website Demo:

Features:
  • Buisnesses can see statistics about their customers and make changes based on trends
  • Buisnesses can create customized forms based on what information they need from their customers
  • Buisnesses can see their upcoming and past appointments
  • Buisnesses can check in their customers
  • Customers can make appointments through platforms they already use (Facebook Messenger, SMS)
  • Customers can get reminded of appointments they have coming up
