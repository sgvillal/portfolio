SWE Envision Outreach (2014-2017)

Society of Women Engineers: Annual Outreach event

"Envision is a free all-day conference held at UCSD that exposes high school girls to career possibilities in science and technology."

Within our outreach event, we strive to place young girls in the shoes of engineers. By connecting them with female engineers, we give them experience from those who have been in their shoes, and walked the path they're on. By engaging them in projects which are major specific, we give them insights into the various depths within the engineering field. In our planning committee we stive to be as hands on and interactive as possible with our activities, so that there is a opporunity to connect and see if the field is the right fit for them.

By involving their parents and having parallel activities with them throughout the day, we strive to keep them fully informed on how to best guide their child and help them reach their full potential.
