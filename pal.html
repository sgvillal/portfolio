PAL: Class Selector - Web App (2016)

In Partnership with Partners at Learning - UCSD Education Department
This was my first project, and first time working with HTML/CSS. Our team worked with the Education Department at UCSD in order to increase interst in the PAL courses, which worked with local K-12 schools.

We worked with our client in order to create a product that was informative and captured attention. Students who were interested in education courses would take this quiz and see what course best fit their needs. Also within the same page they could find out information about all the courses and what they offer.
GitHub Repo Live Website

Features:
  • Colourful UI to capture and retain user attention
  • A point system that tracks what course best suit's the quiz takers needs
  • Ability to see results right away in case quiz cannot be completed
  • Course guides so students feel well informed going into the program. Includes
    • What age group you're teaching
    • What topics are covered in the course
    • Intersting points that may sway choices
Technologies Used:
  • HTML/CSS
  • Javascript
  • Github hosting/version control
