IDEA Engineering Overnight Program

Mentor/Host (2015-2016)
Served as a mentor and resource for high-school seniors admitted to UCSD from underrepresented communities. I provided a personalized experience of our campus by taking my hostee to courses, connecting them to many academic and student resources and sharing my college experience with them for 48 hours.

 By doing so our goal was to provide a glipse into higher education, and show them strategies to enrich their college experience.
