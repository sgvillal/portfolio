Phi Rho Your Boat (2014-2017)

Benefiting the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society
In this fundraiser, teams compete to build the best boat possible to so that their team Captain can race across the pool. The catch is, their boat is completely built out of recyclables which their team collects! With limited time and supplies, teams strive to engineer designs to win prizes such as "Most Creative Design", "Best Structure", ect. At the end the event, all recyclables are sold to fundraise for the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society (LLS).

In the past few years we have worked with LLS to raise both awareness and funds. In the recent years we have begun partnership with "The Man/Women/Kid of the Year", and they have even judged our compeitions.

Working with such passionate people for this cause has been such a enlightening experience, and one that I look forward to year after year. When volunteering I captain a team and we work together to raise money and create a grand ship. For pre-event logistics I also help find sponserships and collect recyclables on campus.
